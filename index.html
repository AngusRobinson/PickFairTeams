Pick Fair Teams

This tool helps you divide a group of people (students, football players etc.) into fair teams based on their individual proficiency levels. For precise information on how it works, see here.

Your data should be entered as follows: two columns without any headers, the left one with a name or ID, and the right one with that person's individual proficiency (e.g. the mark out of 100 they got on the last exam, or your estimated score out of ten for how good a player they are.) It can be uploaded as a CSV file, or you can write the data in manually, or copy and paste from columns in a spreadsheet.
Please click `Reset' before adding new data.
